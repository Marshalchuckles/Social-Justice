Our Vision

We believe in creating economic opportunities for youth across Africa by connecting them with mentors, funding, and the tools they need to succeed in their entrepreneurial journey.

The Social Justice App provides everything you need to turn your business ideas into reality, offering step-by-step guides, access to experts, and continuous support through your startup’s lifecycle.

Mentorship Programs

Our mentorship programs link young entrepreneurs with experienced professionals who can offer valuable guidance. Whether you're just starting or looking to scale, our mentors are here to help.

Funding Opportunities
